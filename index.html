Team Eventia 2021

Eventia

A new, intelligent way to find cool events to attend!

Problems

There is not a wide variety of apps to display events in our country (especially mobile apps). Some people want to go to certain events but do not have other people interested in going with them.

Solutions

Users will be able to find and filter the events they want to participate in according to their preferences (location, date, type, price, mood, etc.). Depending on each person's preferences, you can choose to use a 'buddies generator' with common interests that could accompany you to events.

Customer segments

Students (16-25)
Adults (25-50)
Seniors (50+)
