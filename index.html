Habit Oasis

Problems and solutions

Problems
1. Poor time management caused by stress, work and repetitive actions, accentuated by the current pandemic situation.
2. There is not a wide variety of apps in Romania to help people achieve their goals effectively, by also giving them the option of receiving help from professionals.
Solutions
1. The reminders and sharing features will support the user in pursuing their goal without overloading their minds with information.
2. Users will be able to personalize the way they work on their goals, by also getting more help from life coaches.

Customer Discovery

Problem and solution identification

The problems have been identified by analyzing a Google Form questionnaire's responses from around 40 people from Romania, aged between 15 and 75. This questionnaire created by us (can be found here) addressed questions about each person's lifestyle and personal habits or goals. After collecting their answers and noticing their needs or desires, we came to the conclusion that our habit track app might be just what they need.
Problems
1. As the responses to our questionnaire suggests, 67,4% people forget to do things from time to time and 30,2% people forget to do things often. When life gets busy, people forget to do the things they had planned and they tend to postpone them or give up on them completely.
2. Most common barriers that the people we questioned are facing when it comes to reaching their goals or developing new habits are: lack of time/motivation/organizing, procrastination, comfort, stress or bad habits. All these things keep them from reaching their goals faster or working on them more efficiently.
3. Other apps’ lack of user-friendly interfaces, responsiveness or features for motivating friends/working together with friends.
Solutions
1. Development of a personalized reminders system in order for people to not worry about having to remember everything that they have to do during a day.
2. Constantly reminding users of their goal, the big image of the final result and why they started in the first place. Another way they can be motivated is through small entertaining "rewards", as well as being able to track their progress and see how far they have reached or how little until succeeding, but also being guided by life coaches/personal development coaches through our app. This way the process of reaching their goals is going to be much more simplified and successful.
3. Modern and aesthetically pleasing app, bringing features that can help users in their transformative journeys by connecting more easily with friends: sending them a reminder, a motivational quote or a personalized message, sharing your progress or goals.

Short overview of plan for customer discovery

Our plan for customer discovery consists in creating an online questionnaire with relevant questions for the application we want to build, distributing it to as many people as possible, followed by an analysis and drawing some conclusions from the answers we received.

Detaliation of the used customer discovery plan

All the information was collected through an online questionnaire consisting of 10 questions regarding the concepts of giving up old habits and starting new ones.
This questionnaire was distributed through social media platforms: Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp to a customer segment that is more likely to use technology and mobile applications to accomplish their objectives and it consists of people aged 15-75. After their responses, some insights that we have gained are:
53.5% are happy with their lifestyle and habits, and 46.5% are not
38% want to do more physical exercises, 35% want to have a healthier lifestyle (including eating and sleeping better) and 20% want to be better organised
Changing their lifestyle has been proven difficult for 30% of responders due to external factors (work or studies related busy schedule and the pandemic) and personal reasons (bad time management, lack of motivation and procrastination) for 70%
Over 50% of the responders usually do many repetitive actions and around 30% frequently forget to do a specific action/task
Over 37% are using their phones to remember what to do, 32% are not doing anything special to remember, and the rest are using other methods such as writing on sticky notes, whiteboards or asking their friends to remind them
Around 70% sometimes want to give up a habit, but they don’t know where to start
In order to see more of the varied responses, you can access this link.

The influence of the insights on our product

Our product was slightly modified in terms of functionalities. After some repetitive responses we realized the majority has difficulties in achieving their goals caused by the time management. In that case, our application will have scheduled recurring tasks – more than that, every reminder of the activity will contain a short motivational quote to keep people focused and drive them to complete the task. On the other hand, we are also considering the pandemic situation we are facing – our application will maintain the relationship between people (friends and family). The customers will have the possibility to interact with people, to view their tasks, goals and remind them of the incoming activities.
In general, people want to make changes regarding their lifestyle - our product will take care of the principal factors of healthcare: food, water, sleep, and sport. More than that, we are planning to connect our customers with lifestyle coaches – for any help and suggestions people can directly ask the professionals of this domain.

Customer Validation

Our current overview, based on people's feedback and related to the similar solutions, is that we could move to the customer validation since we identified the target audience and the most useful and desired features that will be implemented. We are planning to have a first simple version of the product in order to receive direct feedback from our users and bring relevant improvements.
